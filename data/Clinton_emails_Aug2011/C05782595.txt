From: OpsNewslIcker Sent: Friday, September 09, 2011 07:32 PM To: NEWS-Egypt; NEWS-Mahogany Cc: SES-0 Subject: Israeli ambassador waiting for flight out of Egypt (AP)
CAIRO - (AP) Egyptian airport officials say the Israeli ambassador is at Cairo's airport waiting for a military plane to evacuate him from the country after protesters broke into Israel's Embassy.
The officials say other Israelis were also waiting for the evacuation flight to take them back to Israel early Saturday. The officials spoke on condition of anonymity because they were not authorized to speak to the media.
